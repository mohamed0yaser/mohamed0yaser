Empon is a multi-flow e-commerce ecosystem designed to connect clients, stores, and businesses in one platform. It combines product shopping, service booking, digital wallet payments, and social features like chat, likes, and comments. Built with Flutter, Empon delivers a scalable, modern experience across Android & iOS.

Core Features

Client Flow

Users can browse products & services, pay via Empon Wallet or cash, chat with friends, and interact through likes & comments.

Store Flow

Store owners manage catalogs, track sales, run ads, and monitor detailed statistics.

Business Flow

Business partners receive and manage customer orders via WhatsApp integration for instant communication.

Empon Wallet

Secure in-app wallet for payments, withdrawals, and balance tracking.

Advertising System

Businesses and stores can promote their products & services through built-in ads.

Social Interaction

Chat with friends, comment on products, and like services, blending commerce with community.

Tech Stack

Flutter (Dart)

Cross-platform development for Android & iOS.

REST APIs

Robust backend APIs for authentication, products, services, and wallet transactions.

Bloc / Cubit

Efficient state management for structured architecture.

Cloud Storage

Used for storing product images, ads, and chat media.
